About Us

Who We Are
Group Various is a Vanuatu-based consultancy firm dedicated to helping investors, entrepreneurs, and businesses navigate Vanuatu’s regulatory and business environment.

Mission: To simplify and facilitate investment, licensing, and business operations in Vanuatu with integrity, efficiency, and professionalism.

Vision: To be the Pacific region’s leading consultancy for business support, financial licensing, and labour mobility coordination.

Core Values:

  • Integrity – Transparency, honesty, confidentiality
  • Efficiency – Meeting deadlines and delivering results
  • Professionalism – High standards in every engagement
  • Collaboration – Working hand-in-hand with clients and authorities

Our Services

Company Incorporation

We facilitate the registration of both local and international companies in Vanuatu. Our services include:

  • Preparation and filing of incorporation documents
  • Compliance with VFSC requirements
  • Guidance on business structure and legal setup

FDL Assistance

We provide comprehensive support to clients applying for a Financial Dealers License:

  • Recruitment and vetting of qualified Local Managers
  • Preparation and certification of application documents
  • Liaison with VFSC throughout the process
  • Local office and compliance setup

Registered Agent for ICs

As an approved Registered Agent, we offer:

  • Compliance monitoring and reporting
  • Document handling and government correspondence
  • Representation for ICs in Vanuatu

Labour Mobility (PALM Scheme)

We assist employers and workers involved in the PALM Scheme by:

  • Partnering with registered Australian farms
  • Coordinating worker recruitment and documentation
  • Supporting both employers and workers throughout the engagement

Consultation & Referrals

Our consultancy services help businesses thrive. We offer:

  • Strategic planning and market entry advice
  • Regulatory compliance consultation
  • Referrals to vetted legal, tax, and industry professionals

Investor Settlement

We help foreign investors settle and operate effectively in Vanuatu:

  • Business registration and licensing
  • Office and team setup
  • Ongoing regulatory and administrative support
